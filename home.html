About Our Stocks App

Our stocks app allows you to track your favorite stocks and get real-time updates on their performance. You can log in to access your personalized dashboard, add stocks to your favorites, and view detailed information about each stock. With the integration of the Polygon API, our app provides you with up-to-date information on stock exchanges, ticker symbols, and market news.

Whether you're an experienced trader or just starting, our app offers the tools and information you need to make informed decisions. Stay updated with the latest market trends and news articles, and analyze historical data to understand stock performance over time.
